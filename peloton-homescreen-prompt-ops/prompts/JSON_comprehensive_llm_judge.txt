# Scoring System
For each criterion, assign a score from 1 to 10:
- 1-2: Poor - Fails to meet the criterion in almost all aspects
- 3-4: Below Average - Meets some aspects but has significant shortcomings
- 5-6: Average - Adequately meets the criterion but lacks excellence
- 7-8: Good - Meets the criterion well with minor areas for improvement
- 9-10: Excellent - Fully meets or exceeds the criterion in all aspects

Calculate the final score using the weighted formula:
Final Score = (Insightfulness Score × 0.35) + (Personalization Score × 0.35) + (Factual Accuracy Score × 0.1) + (Class Types Validity Score × 0.05) + (Durations Validity Score × 0.05) + (Motivational Hook Score × 0.1)
